Life and Palmetto Fellows Scholarships

South Carolina offers two merit-based scholarship opportunities for four-year colleges. The criteria for the scholarships is based on GPA, class ranking (within Faithful Scholars), and SAT/ACT test scores. Please see the SC Commission on Higher Education website for a complete and up to date description of the scholarships.

Life Scholarship Eligibility overview:

  • Score an 1100 on the SAT (24 ACT), 3.0 GPA or higher, ranked in the top 30%

Palmetto Fellows Eligibility overview:

  • Score at least 1200 on the SAT (27 on the ACt), 3.5 GPA or higher, ranked in the top 6% of the class (sophomore, junior, or senior year)
  • Or -- Score at least 1400 on SAT (32 on ACT), 4.0 GPA, no class ranking

If your senior is planning to attend a SC university (public or private) and will meet or exceed the testing requirements for the Life Scholarship, please check the box indicating that you are wanting to be included in the scholarship calculations.

If your student will not be attending college or will be attending an out of state school, please leave the box unchecked.
